Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a story of hope, friendship, and perseverance. It follows the journey of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongfully convicted of murder and his time in Shawshank State Penitentiary.

Actors

The movie features an ensemble cast, including Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding.
